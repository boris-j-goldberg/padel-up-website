Frequently asked questions

Does Padel-Up book courts?

No. Padel-Up only helps you find and coordinate with other players. You (or the group) still book the court directly with the club.

Can anyone join my game?

You control who joins. Players send requests and you (as organizer) approve or reject them. You can also require consent before the game is confirmed.

How do I change my clubs or search area?

Go to your profile or settings → Location. Update your position and radius, then select the clubs you want to see games for.

I found a bug or have a feature idea

Please email us. Early feedback from real players has already shaped the consent and organizer flows.
