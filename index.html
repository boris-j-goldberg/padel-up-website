Find padel partners and organize games.

Padel-Up is a matching app for real games at real clubs. Players set their location and clubs, then create games or post requests. The organizer controls the player list.

Get it on Google Play Download on the App Store

What we do: Help padel players find compatible partners, pick dates, and coordinate games at nearby clubs they actually play at.

What we are not: We do not book courts and we are not club management software.

Our differentiator: Real organizer responsibility + player approval + explicit consent before the game. No more “create and join” chaos or last-minute no-shows.
