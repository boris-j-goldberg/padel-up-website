Privacy Policy

Updated at 2026-03-03

Padel-Up (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by Padel-Up.

This Privacy Policy applies to our website and its associated subdomains (collectively, our “Service”) alongside our mobile application, Padel-Up. By accessing or using our Service, you signify that you have read, understood, and agree to our collection, storage, use, and disclosure of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy.

Definitions

  • Personal Data: any information that directly or indirectly identifies a natural person.
  • Service: the Padel-Up app and related website.
  • You: the individual registered with or using Padel-Up.

What Information Do We Collect?

We collect information when you register, use the app, or contact us. This may include:

  • Name / username
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location data (used only for finding nearby clubs and games)

How Do We Use Your Information?

  • To provide and improve the matching and game organization features
  • To personalize your experience (nearby clubs, game suggestions)
  • To communicate with you about games, requests, and updates
  • To ensure safety and prevent abuse (organizer approval, consent flows)

Do We Share Your Information?

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited information with service providers (hosting, analytics) strictly to operate the service. Game participants see only the information necessary to coordinate the game (name, skill indicators if provided).

Data Retention

We keep your information only as long as needed to provide the service and meet legal obligations. You can request deletion by contacting us or deleting your account.

Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data. Contact us at the email below.

Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your country).

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email support@padel-up.net
